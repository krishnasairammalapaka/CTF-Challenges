ROT13 Decryption Challenge

A flag has been encrypted using the ROT13 cipher.

Your task is to decode the following string to reveal the flag:

SYNF{URYYB_JBEYQ}

Hint: ROT13 is a simple letter substitution cipher where each letter is replaced by the 13th letter after it in the alphabet. Tools like online decoders or command-line utilities can help.

Good luck, and happy CTF-ing!
